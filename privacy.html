Privacy on R Weekly

R Weekly is dedicated to providing privacy on the Internet. To provide you with the choice of opting-out of analytics.

A cookie is a small piece of data sent by a website to your browser. It helps the website to remember information about your visit, like your country and other settings. That can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

We use cookies to deliver content or responses according to the way you use the site, and/or your current context on the site. We do not use cookies to gather or transmit any personally identifiable information about you.
